# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.
Contributors and maintainers are expected to:

- Be respectful in language and behavior.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Accept and provide constructive criticism.
- Keep discussions relevant to improving the project.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, hate speech, personal attacks,
doxxing, threats, or any conduct that creates an unsafe environment.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull
requests, discussions, and other public or private channels used for project
collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues,
or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from
project participation spaces.
